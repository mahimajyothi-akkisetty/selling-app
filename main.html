About Fresh Prawns

Prawns are one of the most popular seafood options worldwide. Not only do they offer a delightful taste, but they also have numerous health benefits. Packed with high-quality protein, vitamins, and minerals, prawns are an excellent choice for those looking to maintain a healthy lifestyle. Our prawns are sourced directly from local farmers and delivered fresh to your doorstep.
